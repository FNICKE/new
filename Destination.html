❄️ Welcome to Kashmir – the "Paradise on Earth"! 🏔️✨

Nestled in the Himalayas, Kashmir offers snow-capped peaks 🏞️, scenic valleys 🌄, and serene lakes like the famous Dal Lake 🛶.

Glide through its waters on a charming shikara ride 🚤 or soak in the beauty of Mughal gardens 🌸. Kashmir is not just a place, it's a feeling. 💙🧳
🏔️ Welcome to Leh-Ladakh – the "Land of High Passes"! 🌌🛣️

A mystical desert in the sky, Ladakh is known for its rugged landscapes 🏜️, crystal-clear lakes, and ancient monasteries 🛕.

Ride through Khardung La 🚵 or witness starlit skies in Nubra Valley 🌠. Ladakh isn’t just a destination — it’s an adventure for the soul. 🧡🌄
🏞️ Welcome to Shimla & Manali – the "Queen of Hills & the Valley of Snow"! 🌨️🏔️

From pine-covered hills 🌲 to Mall Road 🛍️ to snow adventures in Rohtang Pass ⛷️, this duo offers timeless escapes. 💙✨
🛕 Welcome to Amritsar – home of the sacred Golden Temple! 🌟✨

Enjoy peaceful reflection by the Amrit Sarovar 🌊, delicious langar meals 🍛, and the vibrant Wagah Border ceremony 🇮🇳. Amritsar is a soulful experience. 🙏
🌴 Welcome to Kerala – "God's Own Country"! 🚤🌿

Cruise Alleppey's backwaters ⛵ or explore the misty tea hills of Munnar 🍃. Kerala is peace wrapped in green and gold. ✨🌾
